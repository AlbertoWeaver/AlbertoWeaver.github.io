1. How to Take Screenshots Using Keyboard Shortcuts#

Taking screenshots is a useful and relatively easy process on a Lenovo Chromebook. It only takes three steps and it’s also possible to customize your screenshots even further. Here’s a guide on how to take screenshots on your Lenovo Chromebook with keyboard shortcuts.

First of all, press the ‘Ctrl’ and ‘Window Switch’ keys on the upper left part of the keyboard to take a screenshot of your entire screen. It will be saved to the ‘Downloads’ folder as a PNG image file.

The second option is to press ‘Ctrl’ and ‘Shift’ and ‘Window Switch’ keys to take a screenshot of just a part of the screen. After you press the keys, the cursor will change into a crosshair that you can use to drag and select an area. This will also be saved to the ‘Downloads’ folder.

Finally, press ‘Ctrl’, ‘Shift’, and ‘R’ to take a screenshot of the entire screen and copy it into your Chromebook’s clipboard. You can then paste this screenshot into an image editor, document, or other applications.

These steps are simple and easy to do if you want to quickly take a screenshot on your Lenovo Chromebook. You can also customize your screenshots further with the editing tools provided on your Chromebook.
