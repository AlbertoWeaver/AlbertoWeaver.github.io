The second type of screenshot is the partial-page screenshot. This will capture only the area you select instead of the whole page. To take a partial-page screenshot, press the “Ctrl” and “Shift” keys together, then use your mouse to select the area you’d like to capture.

If you need to capture a specific window or application, you can take a window screenshot. To do this, press the “Ctrl” and “Alt” keys together, then select the window or application you’d like to capture. It will be saved to the Downloads folder.

Finally, you can also use the built-in screenshot tool to take a screenshot. This tool allows you to draw shapes, add arrows, and highlight text on the screenshot before you save it. To access this tool, press the “Ctrl” and “Switch Window” keys together, then click the “Capture” button.
