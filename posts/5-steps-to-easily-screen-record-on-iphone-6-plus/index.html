2. Steps to Record Screen on iPhone 6 Plus#

Recording your iPhone’s screen activities is a great way to create content for presentations, tutorials, and other multimedia projects. Here are the five easy steps to record your iPhone 6 Plus screen:

Make sure you have iOS 11 or later on your iPhone 6 Plus. This is the minimum requirement for screen recording feature. Open up the Settings app and find the Control Center option. Tap it, then select Customize Controls. From here, look for the “Screen Recording” option and add it.

Now open Control Center by swiping up from the bottom of your phone’s screen. You should see the Screen Recording icon. Tap the icon and wait a few seconds for the 3-second countdown before it starts recording.

To record audio as well, press and hold the Screen Recording icon and tap the Microphone Audio icon. Now your screen recording will also have audio.

Press the Screen Recording icon again to stop recording. You can find the recorded file in Photos app.
