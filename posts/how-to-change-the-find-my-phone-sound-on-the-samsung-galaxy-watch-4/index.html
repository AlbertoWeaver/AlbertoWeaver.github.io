- Introduction of Find My Phone Feature on Galaxy Watch 4#

The Samsung Galaxy Watch 4 has a great feature that allows users to locate their connected mobile device with a simple press of a button. This feature is known as Find My Phone and once triggered, a sound will be emitted from the mobile device to alert the user of its location. In this tutorial, we will show you how to change the sound used for the Find My Phone feature on the Samsung Galaxy Watch 4.

First, open the Galaxy Wearable app on your connected mobile device. Next, select the Sounds & Vibration menu, then tap on the Find My Phone Sound option. Here you will be able to pick from a variety of sounds that you can set to play when the Find My Phone feature is activated.

Once the desired sound has been chosen, press the Apply button to save your new setting. Finally, the sound for the Find My Phone feature has now been changed on your Galaxy Watch 4. To test it out, press the Find My Phone button on your watch and listen for the newly changed sound.

That’s it! Now you know how to change the Find My Phone sound on the Samsung Galaxy Watch 4. Adjusting this setting can help you to easily and quickly locate your connected device.
