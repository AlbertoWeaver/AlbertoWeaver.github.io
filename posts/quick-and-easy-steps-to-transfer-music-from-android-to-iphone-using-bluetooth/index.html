Bluetooth technology allows you to quickly connect two or more devices. It can also be used to transfer files such as music from one device to another. In this tutorial, we will show you how to transfer music from an Android smartphone to an iPhone using Bluetooth. First, make sure that both devices have their Bluetooth enabled. Then, open the file manager on your Android phone and locate the music files you want to transfer. After that, press the share button and select Bluetooth. Next, navigate to the Bluetooth settings on your iPhone and activate the visible mode to locate the Android device.
