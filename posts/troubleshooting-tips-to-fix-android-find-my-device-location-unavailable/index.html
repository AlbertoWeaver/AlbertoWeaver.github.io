A2: To fix poor signal strength, you can try restarting your device, moving into an area with better signal strength, or connecting to a different network. Depending on the device, you may also be able to enable Wi-Fi calling or LTE-only mode.

A3: To fix GPS connectivity issues, you should look into enabling high accuracy mode on your device, as well as making sure that you have the latest version of Google Maps installed. You should also ensure that your device has the latest version of Google Play Services installed.

A4: To make sure you’re logged in to your Google account, you should go to the Accounts & Privacy Settings in your device’s settings menu. Here, you can check to make sure that your Google account is linked and that you’re signed in.
