1. Steps of Taking Screenshot on iPhone 8#

Taking screenshots is very manageable on an iPhone 8. It can be done quickly with the press of a button. Here are the steps of taking a screenshot on your iPhone 8:

  1. Locate the Sleep/Wake button and the Home button on your iPhone 8.
  2. Press and hold down the Sleep/Wake button and the Home button at the same time.
  3. You will hear the sound of a camera shutter and see a white screen for a moment. This means that the screenshot has been taken.
  4. Your screenshot will be saved in the Screenshots folder of the Photos app.
