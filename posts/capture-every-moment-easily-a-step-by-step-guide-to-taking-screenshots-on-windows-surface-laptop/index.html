1. Introduction to Taking Screenshots on Windows Surface Laptop#

Taking screenshots on Windows Surface Laptop is easy and quick. To capture a screenshot and save it as an image file, press the Windows logo key + the Volume down button at the same time. The screen will dim briefly and you will hear a shutter sound, which confirms that the screenshot has been taken. The screenshot will be automatically saved in the Screenshots folder inside the Pictures folder. You can find it there or access it from the Photos app. You can also copy and paste the screenshot into an email message or document. To take a screenshot of only an active window, hold the Alt key and press the Windows logo key + the Volume down button. This will capture the active window and open a dialog box, where you can save or share the screenshot.

2. Steps for Taking a Screenshot on Windows Surface Laptop#

Taking screenshots on a Windows Surface laptop is easy and straightforward. To take a screenshot using the built-in hardware, simply press and hold the Windows and Volume Down keys on your laptop at the same time. The screen will briefly dim to indicate that a screenshot has been taken. The screenshot will be automatically saved to the Screenshots folder in the Pictures folder. Alternatively, you may also use the Snipping Tool in Windows 10 to take screenshots at any user-defined region. All you need to do is open the Snipping Tool application, click New, and use the mouse to draw a region of the desired size. Once the region is drawn, a screenshot of the window will be taken and saved in the Pictures folder. In conclusion, it is very easy to take screenshots on a Windows Surface laptop using either the hardware or Snipping Tool.

2. Step by Step Guide to Capturing Screenshots#

Capturing a screenshot on a Windows Surface Laptop has never been easier. With just a few simple steps, you can capture any moment in time. Here’s how to do it:

First, locate the “PrtScn” key on your laptop’s keyboard. This is typically located in the upper right corner of the laptop’s keyboard. Once located, press and hold the “PrtScn” button.

Second, open the “Snipping Tool” program. This program can be found in the “Accessories” menu of the Start Menu. Once opened, select the type of screenshot you would like to take. You can choose from a free-form snip, rectangular snip, window snip or full screen snip.

Third, select the area of your screen that you would like to capture. Once you have chosen the area, press the “Ctrl” and “PrtScn” keys together to capture the screenshot. The captured screenshot will be saved in your Pictures folder.

Finally, to open the image, simply press the “Windows” key + “E” to open the File Explorer. Then, navigate to your Pictures folder and you can easily view the screenshot you captured. Congratulations! You’ve just captured a moment in time with your Windows Surface Laptop.
