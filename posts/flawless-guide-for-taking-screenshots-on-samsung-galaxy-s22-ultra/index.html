1. Cara Mengambil Screenshot Dengan Tombol Fisik#

Taking screenshots on the Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra is a snap. The easiest way to do it is by using the physical buttons. All you need to do is press and hold the Power and Volume Down buttons simultaneously and a screenshot will be taken. Your phone will vibrate and the display will show a screenshot animation to let you know that a screenshot has been taken.

You can then find the screenshot in the Gallery app or the Files app — it will be saved in the Screenshots folder. You can also review, edit, and share your screenshots from inside the Gallery app. You can crop, rotate, annotate, and add text or drawings to it.

For added convenience, the Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra also supports screenshots with a gesture. You can swipe three fingers on the screen to take a screenshot, which is perfect for taking screenshots of longer content that stretches beyond the edge of the display.

Finally, you can also take screenshots from within supported apps. For example, you will find the “Screenshot” button in the YouTube app, which you can tap to take and save a screenshot of the currently playing video.

Whatever method you use, taking screenshots on the Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra is hassle-free.

2. Menggunakan Aplikasi Aksesibilitas untuk Mengambil Screenshot#

Taking a screenshot on Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra is as easy as pressing a few buttons. To take a screenshot, simply press and hold the ‘Volume Down’ button and the ‘Power’ button together. You should see a flash of white light along the edge of the screen and hear a shutter sound. Your screenshot has now been taken and saved on your device. You can find it in your Gallery or Photos app. This method is the simplest way to take a screenshot on the Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra.

3. Cara Mengambil Screenshot Menggunakan Aplikasi Lainnya#

Taking screenshots on your Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra is easy with the physical buttons. The most common way to take a screenshot on the S22 Ultra is to press the Volume Down and Power buttons at the same time. This should take a screenshot and save it to your Gallery app. You can also use the same method to take a scrolling screenshot, which allows you to capture a long page in a single image. This can be done by pressing the Volume Down and Power buttons and then tapping the Scroll Capture button on the screen. The scrolling screenshot will then be captured and saved to your Gallery.

4. Bagaimana Cara Berbagi dan Menyimpan Screenshot#

The Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra is a great phone for capturing screenshots. Taking screenshots with the Galaxy S22 Ultra is easy and simple. To take a screenshot using the physical buttons, all you need to do is press and hold the Power and Volume Down buttons together. Then, you can release the buttons when you see the screenshot animation. Your screenshot will be saved in the Screenshot folder in your Gallery.

If you need to take a screenshot in an app, you can also use the Side Key panel. Swipe on the Side Key panel to the left. Tap on the Screenshot option and the screenshot will be taken. Again, the screenshot is saved in the Screenshot folder in your Gallery.

You can also take a longer screenshot. To do this, tap on the scroll capture icon after taking a screenshot with any of the methods above. Then, scroll down and tap on the Capture button until you have captured the entire page. The long screenshot will be saved as an image in the same folder.

Finally, you can use the built-in Bixby voice assistant to take a screenshot. First, press and hold the Bixby button. Then, say or type in “Take a Screenshoot” and the screenshot will be taken and saved.
