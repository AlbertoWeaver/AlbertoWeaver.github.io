3. Update the Android Version#

Updating your Samsung Galaxy J2 to the latest Android version is simple. First, open the Settings app and then find the About Phone option. Tap it and then select Software Update. Your device will then check for available updates. If an update is available, tap Download now to start the process. Once the download is complete, tap Install now to apply the update. Your device will then restart and the latest version of Android will be installed. That’s it! With just a few taps, your Samsung Galaxy J2 is now up to date.
