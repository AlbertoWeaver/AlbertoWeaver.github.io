1. Set Up Google Account On Both Android Devices#

Setting up your Google account on both devices is the first step in transferring contacts from one Android device to another. All you need is a working Gmail account. To set it up on your devices, open the Settings menu and look for Accounts and Sync. Tap the “Add an Account” option and select “Google”. Enter your Gmail credentials and you’re set up. You may get a prompt to synchronize your contacts; if you accept it, your contacts will be backed up to your Google account. On the other device, repeat the same steps to set up your Gmail account. Once you do that, your contacts will sync to the new device.
