1. Introduction to Steam Screenshot Folder#

Having a new Steam screenshot folder can help you organize your screenshots better. If you’re a frequent Steam user and take a lot of screenshots, changing the Steam screenshot folder can be a great way to organize your screenshots more efficiently. Here’s a step-by-step guide to change your Steam screenshot folder:

  1. Launch Steam on your computer.
  2. Once it’s open, go to the Steam menu in the top left corner and choose the Settings option.
  3. On the Settings page, go to the In-Game tab and select the “Screenshot Folder” option.
  4. Select the folder you want to use for screenshots.
  5. Once you’ve chosen your folder, click on the “Select” button to save the changes.

That’s it! You’ve successfully changed your Steam screenshot folder and are now ready to save all your screenshots in one place. Organizing your screenshots has never been easier!
